How To Register for Glitch 2.0

1. Incase you haven't logged in before, sign in as a VITian or as a Non-VITian (if you aren't a student/faculty of VIT).
2. Login to the portal and find us in the Technical Events tab of the Events Registration section.
3. Click on the Register button beside the event name Glitch 2.0
4. Complete the payment process by going to the View Pending Payments tab in the Payments section.
5. After completion of payment, your registeration will be successful.
