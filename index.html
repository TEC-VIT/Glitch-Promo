About Glitch

GLITCH 2.0 is a one of a kind online cryptic-tech hunt which will test your eye for detail and also your technical and problem-solving skills. There will be four rounds, of increasing difficulty. All the three rounds will have an overarching technological storyline which will be unveiled as you progress through the rounds. In each round, you will be posed with challenging questions/puzzles based on multi-disciplinary technical fields which needs to be decoded and solved within the provided time limit.

The event does not need any special skills and thus, both novices and experts can participate freely. You will be given scores based on your ability to decode and solve the challenges and the teams with the highest score will qualify for the subsequent rounds.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do i need prior coding knowledge to participate?
The questions will focus on the participants basic logical reasoning skills and problem solving capabilities, hence both novice and experts can participate.
Do i need to have any paid softwares to solve the challenges?
The questions asked in the challenges do not require any paid softwares to be solved.
Is it a team event? If yes, how many people can be in a team?
We believe that all participants may have their own unique skill set and hence working as a team will prove to be an engaging and thrilling experience, team size: 2- 4 members.
What if I don't have a team?
We will have tons of online activities and discussion forums before the event starts for you to interact with other participants and form teams, however, if you do not have a team, do not worry, we will pair you up with other participants.
Can students outside of VIT participate?
Definitely! We love external participation! All are welcome to register for this event.
How many rounds is the event spread through?
The event takes place within 4 rounds. Elimination begins at round two.
Do i need experience or familiarity with technologies in questions?
Basic knowledge of scientific theories and fundamentals is enough to get you started. These questions will mainly call for problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
What if I lose my internet connectivity in the middle of the hunt?
Although this is an online cryptic-hunt, you do not need to be connected to the challenge at all times. You can continue your work on decoding the questions while getting your internet connectivity back.
Can I refer to online sources for answers?
Yes absolutely! You are free to use any and all resources that the glorious world of the internet has to offer.
What is the scoring criteria?
Points would be added for correctly answering questions. Final scores would be relative.
What are the qualifying criteria for the different stages?
The rules and regulations regarding the details of the event will be out shortly. Please refer to them for more clarity on the structure of the event.
